The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bleach

"Bleach" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo. The story follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager with the ability to see ghosts. One day, he encounters a Soul Reaper named Rukia Kuchiki, who is injured while protecting Ichigo's family from a Hollow, a malevolent lost soul. In order to save his family and herself, Rukia transfers her Soul Reaper powers to Ichigo, turning him into a substitute Soul Reaper.
As Ichigo begins his duties of protecting humans from evil spirits and guiding souls to the afterlife, he discovers a vast spiritual world filled with various factions and enemies, including the Soul Society, a realm where Soul Reapers reside, and the mysterious organization known as the Arrancars. Alongside his friends and allies, including other spiritually aware humans, Soul Reapers, and even former enemies, Ichigo embarks on numerous adventures, battles powerful foes, and uncovers the secrets of his own past.
Throughout its run, "Bleach" explores themes of friendship, duty, and the consequences of power. It features intense action sequences, intricate world-building, and complex character development. The series has garnered a large fanbase and has been adapted into an anime, movies, and various other media formats.

Bungo stray dog

"Bungo Stray Dogs" is a Japanese manga series written by Kafka Asagiri and illustrated by Sango Harukawa. The story revolves around individuals with supernatural abilities and is set in a fictional version of Yokohama, Japan. These individuals are often referred to as "Ability Users."
The protagonist, Atsushi Nakajima, is an orphan who was kicked out of his orphanage and is struggling to survive on the streets. One day, he encounters members of the Armed Detective Agency, a group of Ability Users who use their powers to solve various cases and mysteries. Atsushi learns that he possesses a unique ability called "Beast Beneath the Moonlight," which allows him to transform into a powerful white tiger.
As Atsushi becomes involved with the Armed Detective Agency, he forms bonds with its members, including Dazai Osamu, a former member of the Port Mafia, and other colorful characters inspired by famous literary figures. Together, they face off against various criminal organizations, including the Port Mafia, a ruthless group of Ability Users led by powerful leaders like Ryunosuke Akutagawa and Ougai Mori.
Throughout the series, "Bungo Stray Dogs" explores themes of identity, friendship, and the struggle between good and evil. It combines elements of action, mystery, and comedy, with intricate character backstories and literary references woven into the narrative. The series has been adapted into multiple anime seasons, novels, and other media.

jujutsu kaisen

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with exceptional physical abilities and a strong sense of justice. When Yuji discovers a cursed object, a rotting finger, he inadvertently releases a curse into the world. To protect his friends and family from harm, Yuji decides to consume the finger, becoming the host of a powerful curse named Sukuna.
As a result, Yuji is recruited by the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College, a school for Jujutsu Sorcerers who combat curses and supernatural threats. Alongside his classmates, including the stoic Megumi Fushiguro and the cheerful Nobara Kugisaki, Yuji trains to control his newfound powers and eliminate curses threatening humanity.
Throughout their journey, Yuji and his friends encounter various curses, powerful sorcerers, and mysterious organizations with their own agendas. As they delve deeper into the world of curses, they uncover dark secrets about the nature of their powers and the true intentions of those around them.
"Jujutsu Kaisen" features intense action scenes, intricate world-building, and complex characters grappling with themes of morality, sacrifice, and the consequences of power. The series has gained widespread popularity for its compelling storylines, dynamic characters, and stunning artwork.
